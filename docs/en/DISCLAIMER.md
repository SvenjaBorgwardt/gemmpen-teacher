## The results are suggestions, not finished grades

The app reads handwriting and uses an AI model to produce suggestions: recognised text, scores, reasons, quotations and feedback. Such AI output can be wrong, incomplete or biased. This applies to the handwriting recognition just as much as to the scores and reasons.

Therefore, without exception:

- Always review recognised text, scores, reasons, quotations and feedback yourself before relying on them.
- The app is an aid and a draft. It does not replace your own professional assessment.
- The educational and legal responsibility for grades, feedback and any resulting decisions rests solely with the assessing teacher.
- Do not use the output as the sole basis for report-card grades, promotion or examination decisions, or any other legally significant decision.
